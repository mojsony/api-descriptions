Bill Presentment API Enumerations
===============

Bill Statuses
--------------

Statuses of the bill

Literal           | Description
------------------|------------------------
all      | All
paid     | Paid
not-paid | Not paid
pending  | Pending

Subscription Statuses
----------

Statuses of bill subscription

Literal           | Description
------------------|------------------------
pending | Pending
active  | Active
canceled| Canceled
closed  | Closed

Issuing Periods
--------------

Issuing period of bill. Commonly used value is `monthly`

Literal           | Description
------------------|------------------------
monthly   | Monthly
yearly    | Yearly
quarterly | Quarterly
